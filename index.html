Philosophy of Education


As Nelson Mandela’s quote says “Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world”. Education is indeed a weapon that you can shield into your existence here. Education for me is a gift that no one will ever take away from you because you earned that thing. You worked tirelessly day and night to review your lessons and finish your schoolwork. That’s a thing that we aim for because we want to achieve something in love. We have a goal that we are working hard to reach. Dreams seem impossible but if you close enough, in the blink of an eye, it’s right in our eyes.


Education is a broad thing that is astonishing to see. It brings out the best of us. It will let us realize that each one of us has a different perception of something. And that’s okay. It will let us realize that there is a deeper meaning to something if we think it through. One thing can lead to a thousand possible realities. We learn new things every day. We acquire things that we see and hear from other people. I don’t fear change. If that’s a thing that will make me a better person, I will gladly embrace it. Through learning, I can express myself, think brighter and broader, realize something that I never knew before, make me stronger, and positively see that the future is bright because every new learning is a blessing. Not only for me but for all of us.


As said, change is constant. We should let our imaginations run wild. Live with an open mind and open heart and lots of hope. And finally see the endless possibilities of life.
